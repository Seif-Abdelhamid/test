About This Sauce

Our Hot sauce packs a flavorful kick with aged cayenne and red pepper, balanced by a tangy hint of vinegar and a touch of garlic. It's crafted to deliver just the right amount of heat with a smooth, rich texture that keeps you coming back for more! Certainly the spiciest of all our sauces but one of the most flavorful! Goes well on everything.
